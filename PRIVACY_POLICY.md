# Privacy Policy for AuraWidgets

**Last updated: April 3, 2026**

AuraWidgets ("we", "our", or "the app") is a home screen widget app for iPhone and iPad. This Privacy Policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and what we do not do with it.

---

## 1. Data We Access

### Location
- **What:** Your approximate or precise location when you open the app.
- **Why:** To display local weather conditions in the Weather widget.
- **How:** Location is requested only while the app is in use (`NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription`). We do not track your location in the background.
- **Stored:** Location coordinates are used temporarily to fetch weather data and are never stored beyond the current session.

### Calendar Events
- **What:** Upcoming calendar events from your device.
- **Why:** To display your schedule in the Calendar widget.
- **How:** Events are read directly from your device's EventKit store. Only event titles and dates are displayed — no event details, attendees, or notes are accessed.
- **Stored:** Calendar data is never stored outside your device.

---

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect any personal information.
- We do **not** have servers, databases, or accounts.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties.
- We do **not** send any data off your device.

All data (your todos, habits, countdowns, quotes, and widget settings) is stored **only on your device** using Apple's UserDefaults and is never transmitted anywhere.

---

## 3. Weather Data

Weather information is retrieved using **Apple WeatherKit**, Apple's own weather service. Any data sent to WeatherKit (your location) is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). AuraWidgets does not operate or control the WeatherKit service.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

AuraWidgets does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, and no data collection of any kind.

---

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
